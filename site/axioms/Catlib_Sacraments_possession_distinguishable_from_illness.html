Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 6 (§1673): Possession is distinguishable from illness. "Illness, especially psychological illness, is a very different matter; treating this is the concern of medical science." demonic possession from mental illness. The Catechism asserts the distinction but gives NO diagnostic criteria. This is perhaps the biggest epistemic gap in the exorcism teaching.
