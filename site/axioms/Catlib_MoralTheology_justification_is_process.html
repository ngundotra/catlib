Lean Declaration

axiom justification_is_process :
  -- Justification involves multiple stages:
  -- conversion → forgiveness → purification → healing
  -- (not a single event but an unfolding transformation)
  True
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 4 (§1989-1990): Justification is a process. "The first work of grace is conversion" (§1989) followed by purification, reconciliation, freedom, and healing (§1990). a single moment of being declared righteous. This is why Catholics talk about "being justified" as an ongoing state, while Protestants talk about "the moment of justification."
