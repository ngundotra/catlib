opaque deliberatelyFrustratesProcreation : ConjugalAct → Prop
❅

Why This Axiom?

Whether a conjugal act deliberately frustrates one of its meanings. This is the key distinction: NFP does NOT deliberately frustrate procreation (the couple simply abstains during fertile times). Contraception DOES deliberately frustrate it (the act is performed while procreation is actively rendered impossible). This predicate captures the "by human initiative" clause of HV §12.
