Eucharist.christ_substance_is_whole_christ

axiom — Catlib/Sacraments/Eucharist.lean:413

Catholic

⁂

Lean Declaration

axiom christ_substance_is_whole_christ :
  ∀ (species : EucharisticSpecies),
    species.substance = EucharisticSubstance.christ →
    Christology.wholeChristPresent
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (§1374-§1376): When the substance is Christ, the WHOLE Christ is present — body, blood, soul, and divinity.

Source: [Tradition] Council of Trent, Session XIII, Canon 1;
