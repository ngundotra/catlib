Lean Declaration

axiom philia_symmetric :
  ∀ (tl : TypedLove),
    tl.kind = LoveKind.philia →
    ∃ (tl' : TypedLove),
      tl'.kind = LoveKind.philia ∧
      tl'.lover = tl.beloved ∧
      tl'.beloved = tl.lover ∧
      tl'.degree > 0
❅

Why This Axiom?

Philia (friendship) is symmetric: if A has philia toward B, then B has philia toward A.

Source: [Philosophy] Aristotle NE VIII.2; Aquinas ST II-II q.23 a.1.
