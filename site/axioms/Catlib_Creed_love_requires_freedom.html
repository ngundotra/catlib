Lean Declaration

axiom love_requires_freedom :
  ∀ (p : Person), p.hasFreeWill = false →
    ¬ inCommunion (.person p) .god
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 1 (§1033): Union with God requires freely choosing to love him. this but doesn't argue for it. One could ask: can a determined being love? The Catechism says no — but that's a philosophical commitment, not a derivation.
