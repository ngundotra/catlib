Eucharist.eucharist_preserves_guilt_state

axiom — Catlib/Sacraments/Eucharist.lean:729

Catholic

⁂

Lean Declaration

axiom eucharist_preserves_guilt_state :
  ∀ (sp : SinProfile),
    (eucharistEffectOnProfile sp).guilt = sp.guilt
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (§1395): Communion does NOT remove guilt (Layer 2). "The Eucharist is not ordered to the forgiveness of mortal sins — that is proper to the sacrament of Reconciliation."

Source: [Definition] CCC §1395.
