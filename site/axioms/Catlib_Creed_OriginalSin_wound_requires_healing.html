Lean Declaration

axiom wound_requires_healing :
  ∀ (p : Person),
    natureIsWounded p →
    ¬ canReachSupernaturalEnd p AnthropologicalState.Fallen →
    -- External help is needed: there exists a grace that, if given,
    -- would heal the wound
    ∃ (g : Grace), graceGiven p g → graceHealsWound g p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 3 (CCC §405, 1708; Council of Trent Session 6 ch.1): WOUND REQUIRES HEALING. A wounded nature cannot reach its supernatural end without external help. The wound of original sin is not self-healing — no amount of natural effort can restore what was lost.

Source: [Tradition] Council of Trent Session 6, ch. 1;
