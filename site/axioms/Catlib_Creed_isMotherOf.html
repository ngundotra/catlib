Lean Declaration

opaque isMotherOf : HumanPerson → IncarnateSubject → Prop
❅

Why This Axiom?

Whether a human person is the mother of another (including all their natures). This is what Ephesus (431) affirmed: motherhood targets the PERSON, not a nature in isolation. If you bore a divine person, you are the mother of God — not merely "mother of Christ's human nature."
