Lean Declaration

axiom authority_requires_higher_source :
  ∀ (a : SpiritualAuthority),
    a.domain = "exorcism" → a.holder = a.source → False
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM: Spiritual authority must come from above — you cannot be your own source of spiritual authority. Heb 5:4 ("no one takes this honor on himself"). The chain is hierarchical by design.

Source: [Scripture] Jn 15:5 ("without me you can do nothing"),

Hidden assumption: Authority is not democratically distributed.
