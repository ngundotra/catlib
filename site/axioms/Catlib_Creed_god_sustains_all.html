axiom god_sustains_all :
  ∀ (s : SoulState), s.sustained
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (§301, Col 1:17): God sustains ALL that exists. Heb 1:3 ("sustaining all things by his powerful word"), Acts 17:28 ("in him we live and move and have our being"). conservation of being. Even annihilationists agree God sustains things while they exist; they just think God can withdraw this.

Source: [Scripture] Col 1:17 ("in him all things hold together"),
