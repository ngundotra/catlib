Lean Declaration

axiom imago_dei :
  ∀ (p : Person), p.hasIntellect = true → isImageOfGod p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (Gen 1:27; CCC §1700): IMAGO DEI. Humans are made in God's image. Every person with intellect bears the image of God as an ontological property — it is constitutive, not acquired.

Source: [Scripture] Gen 1:27 ("God created man in his own image").
