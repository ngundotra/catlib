Lean Declaration

axiom communion_not_self_reflexive :
  ∀ (p : Person), p.isMoralAgent = true → ¬ inCommunion (.person p) (.person p)
❅

Why This Axiom?

Persons are not in communion with themselves — communion requires an other.
