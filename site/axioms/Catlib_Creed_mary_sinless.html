Catholic

⁂

Lean Declaration

axiom mary_sinless :
  ¬committedPersonalSin mary
❅

Why This Axiom?

Mary never committed personal sin either. This is a STRONGER claim than the Immaculate Conception (which is about original sin only). The Catechism holds Mary was free from ALL sin.

Source: CCC §493; Council of Trent, Session 6, Canon 23.
