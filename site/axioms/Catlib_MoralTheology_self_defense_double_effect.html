Lean Declaration

axiom self_defense_double_effect :
  ∀ (act : DoubleEffectAct),
    act.goodEffect → act.badEffect →
    act.intendsGood →
    -- The act has genuine double effect
    act.intendsGood
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 1 (§2263): Self-defense has double effect. "The act of self-defense can have a double effect: the preservation of one's own life; and the killing of the aggressor."

Source: [Tradition] Aquinas ST II-II q.64 a.7
