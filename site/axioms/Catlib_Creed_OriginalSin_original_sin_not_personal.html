Lean Declaration

axiom original_sin_not_personal :
  ∀ (p : Person),
    p.hasIntellect = true →
    natureIsWounded p ∧ ¬ isPersonalFault p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (CCC §404-405): ORIGINAL SIN IS INHERITED, NOT PERSONAL. Original sin is transmitted to all humans as a wounded condition, not as personal guilt. You did not commit Adam's sin; you inherit its consequences.

Source: [Definition] CCC §404-405.
