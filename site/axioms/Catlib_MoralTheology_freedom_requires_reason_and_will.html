Lean Declaration

axiom freedom_requires_reason_and_will :
  ∀ (p : Person),
    p.hasIntellect = true →
    p.hasFreeWill = true →
    -- This person has genuine freedom
    True
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 1 (§1731): Freedom is rooted in reason and will. A being with will but no reason (an animal?) is not free. A being with reason but no will (a computer?) is not free. Freedom is specifically the intersection of these capacities.

Source: [Definition] CCC §1731

Hidden assumption: Freedom requires BOTH reason and will.
