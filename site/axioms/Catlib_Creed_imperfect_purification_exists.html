Lean Declaration

axiom imperfect_purification_exists :
  ∃ (p : Person), diedInGrace p ∧ ¬fullyPurified p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 2 (§1030): A middle state exists. Some die in grace but imperfectly purified — they chose God, but still carry impurity. is a spectrum, not binary. If holiness were binary, you'd either be purified or not — no middle ground. DENOMINATIONAL: Catholic only. Protestants hold that Christ's righteousness covers the believer completely at justification (simul justus et peccator), so no further purification is needed.
