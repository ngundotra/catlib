Lean Declaration

axiom death_separates :
  ∀ (p : HumanPerson),
    isDead p →
    ¬hasCorporealAspect p ∧ hasSpiritualAspect p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 4 (§997): Death separates body from soul. The body decays. The soul persists. The person is INCOMPLETE.

Source: [Definition] CCC §997.
