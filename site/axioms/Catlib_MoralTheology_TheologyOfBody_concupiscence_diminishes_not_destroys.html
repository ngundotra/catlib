Lean Declaration

axiom concupiscence_diminishes_not_destroys :
  ∀ (nc : NuptialCapacity),
    -- In the Fallen state, capacity is diminished...
    nc.level > 0 →
    -- ...but the ability to choose self-gift remains
    nc.canChooseSelfGift
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (TOB 26:5, 29:4; CCC §405): CONCUPISCENCE DIMINISHES, NOT DESTROYS. In the Fallen state, nuptial capacity is reduced but non-zero. Concupiscence (the disordering of desires after the Fall) weakens but does not eliminate the capacity for authentic self-gift.
