Lean Declaration

axiom primary_secondary_non_competing :
  ∀ (_e : CausedEvent) (level : CausalLevel),
    -- Both levels of causation apply simultaneously
    -- without diminishing either
    level = CausalLevel.primary ∨ level = CausalLevel.secondary
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 4 (§308): God operates in and through secondary causes. "God is at work in you, both to will and to work for his good pleasure." God causing something at the primary level doesn't prevent the creature from genuinely causing it at the secondary level. This is a NON-STANDARD causation model — normally if A causes X and B causes X, they're competitors. Here they're not.

Source: [Definition] CCC §308; [Scripture] Phil 2:13

Hidden assumption: Primary and secondary causation don't compete.
