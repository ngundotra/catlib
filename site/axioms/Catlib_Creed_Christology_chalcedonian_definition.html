axiom chalcedonian_definition :
  naturesRemainDistinct christ ∧
  naturesUnchanged christ ∧
  naturesUndivided christ ∧
  naturesInseparable christ
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (§467): The four Chalcedonian negatives hold for Christ. "acknowledged in two natures without confusion, without change, without division, without separation."

Source: [Tradition] Council of Chalcedon (451); [Definition] CCC §467.
