Lean Declaration

axiom mortal_sin_causes_loss_of_grace :
  ∀ (s : Sin) (state : SpiritualState),
    s.isMortal →
    state.graceState = GraceState.inGrace →
    ∃ (newState : SpiritualState),
      newState.person = state.person ∧
      newState.graceState = GraceState.notInGrace
❅

Why This Axiom?

§1861 as an AXIOM: Mortal sin causes loss of grace. This is presented as a fact, not derived from anything. We must axiomatize it because the Catechism doesn't give a mechanism — it's a revealed truth, not a reasoned conclusion.
