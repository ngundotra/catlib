Why This Axiom?

AXIOM: Christ delegates authority to FORGIVE SINS to the Apostles. If you forgive the sins of any, they are forgiven them; if you retain the sins of any, they are retained."). sacramental delegation. Protestants read it as a general commission to proclaim the gospel of forgiveness. NOTE: Priests forgive IN CHRIST'S NAME, not by their own authority. They are instrumental causes, not principal causes.

Source: [Scripture] Jn 20:21-23 ("Receive the Holy Spirit.
