Lean Declaration

axiom rational_accessibility :
  ∀ (p : MoralPrecept) (person : Person),
    person.hasIntellect = true →
    -- The person CAN (in principle) discover this precept
    p.accessibleToReason
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 2 (§1954): Rational accessibility — reason alone can discover the natural law. But what counts as reason? If two people reason honestly and disagree, the theory says one of them is failing to reason properly. That's a strong claim the text never makes explicit.

Source: [Definition] CCC §1954

Hidden assumption: "Reason" is sufficient for moral knowledge.
