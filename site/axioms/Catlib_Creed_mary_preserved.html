Why This Axiom?

AXIOM: mary_preserved — Mary was preserved from original sin. This is the actual doctrinal commitment of the Immaculate Conception, defined ex cathedra by Pius IX in Ineffabilis Deus (1854).

Source: CCC §491-492; Pius IX, Ineffabilis Deus (1854).
