axiom — Catlib/Creed/Purgatory.lean:146

Catholic

⁂

Lean Declaration

axiom post_mortem_purification :
  ∀ (p : Person), diedInGrace p → ¬fullyPurified p → inPurgatory p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 3 (§1030): Post-mortem purification occurs. Those who die in grace but imperfectly purified undergo purification after death to achieve the holiness necessary for heaven. HIDDEN ASSUMPTION 1: Post-mortem change is possible. This is in TENSION with Hell.lean's `death_is_final` axiom! HIDDEN ASSUMPTION 2: There is a temporal process after death. How does time work for a disembodied soul? The Catechism never addresses this.
