Lean Declaration

axiom holiness_required_for_heaven :
  ∀ (p : Person), attainsBeatificVision p → fullyPurified p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 1 (§1030 + Rev 21:27): Nothing impure can enter heaven. Holiness at or above the threshold is required for the beatific vision. Also: Heb 12:14 — "without holiness no one will see the Lord." DENOMINATIONAL: Ecumenical — Protestants agree holiness is required for heaven. They disagree about how it is achieved.

Source: [Scripture] Rev 21:27 — "Nothing unclean will enter it."
