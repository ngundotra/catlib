Lean Declaration

axiom good_increases_freedom :
  ∀ (fd : FreedomDegree),
    fd.orientedToGood →
    -- Freedom level increases
    -- (choosing good makes you freer)
    fd.level > 0
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (§1733): Doing good increases freedom. "The more one does what is good, the freer one becomes." a direction. It's not neutral between good and evil choices. This contradicts the common modern assumption that freedom is just "having options." Under the Catechism, some options make you MORE free and others make you LESS free.
