Lean Declaration

axiom freedom_is_libertarian :
  ∀ (p : Person) (choice : FreeChoice Prop),
    p.hasFreeWill = true →
    choice.alternativesExist
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 5 (implicit): The freedom model is libertarian. the person must have been able to genuinely choose otherwise. Under compatibilism, "self-exclusion" is weaker — the person acted from their desires but may not have been able to want differently. The Catechism's framing only works with libertarian free will.
