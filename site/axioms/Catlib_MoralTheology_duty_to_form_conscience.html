Lean Declaration

axiom duty_to_form_conscience :
  ∀ (p : Person),
    p.hasIntellect = true →
    -- The person has a duty to seek moral truth
    -- (failure to do so makes subsequent ignorance culpable)
    True
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 5 (§1778, implicit): Duty to form conscience. not just follow whatever your conscience currently says. This is what makes culpable ignorance possible — if there were no duty to inquire, all ignorance would be innocent.

Source: [Definition] CCC §1783-1785

Hidden assumption: You have a positive obligation to SEEK truth,
