Lean Declaration

axiom charity_can_increase :
  ∀ (tl : TypedLove),
    tl.kind = LoveKind.agape →
    ∃ (tl' : TypedLove),
      tl'.kind = LoveKind.agape ∧
      tl'.lover = tl.lover ∧
      tl'.beloved = tl.beloved ∧
      tl'.degree > tl.degree
❅

Why This Axiom?

Charity can increase: virtuous acts increase the degree of agape.

Source: [Definition] CCC §1827.
