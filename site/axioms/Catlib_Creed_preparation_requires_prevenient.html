Lean Declaration

axiom preparation_requires_prevenient :
  ∀ (r : Receptivity),
    r.canReceiveSanctifying → r.hasPrevenient
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 1 (§2001): Preparation for sanctifying grace requires prevenient grace. "The preparation of man for the reception of grace is already a work of grace."

Source: [Definition] CCC §2001
