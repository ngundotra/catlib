Lean Declaration

axiom both_aspects_in_life :
  ∀ (p : HumanPerson),
    ¬isDead p →
    ¬isRisen p →
    hasCorporealAspect p ∧ hasSpiritualAspect p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM 1 (§362): A living person has BOTH aspects. "The human person is a being at once corporeal and spiritual."

Source: [Scripture] Gen 2:7; [Definition] CCC §362.
