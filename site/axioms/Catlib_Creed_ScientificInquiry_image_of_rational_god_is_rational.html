axiom image_of_rational_god_is_rational :
  ∀ (p : Person),
    isImageOfGod p →
    godCreatesThruWisdom →
    imageSharesRationality p
❅

Why This Axiom?

AXIOM (§1700, Gen 1:27 + §299): The image of a rational God shares in that rationality.

Source: [Definition] CCC §1700 + §299; [Philosophy] Aquinas.
