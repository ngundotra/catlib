# Axiom Analysis: Tracing Every Assumption to Its Source

Across 12 formalizations, we declared **44 axioms**. This document
categorizes each by its ultimate source and identifies which are
truly foundational vs. potentially derivable from deeper principles.

## The goal

Reduce 44 axioms to the smallest possible base set, where each
base axiom is grounded in Scripture, Sacred Tradition, or Natural
Law reasoning. The Catechism itself cites these three sources —
we should be able to trace every assumption back to one of them.

---

## Proposed reduction: 44 → ~15 base axioms

### Philosophical base (5 axioms)

```
P1. HYLOMORPHISM: Reality is composed of form and matter.
    (Grounds: soul-as-form, anti-dualism, single nature)

P2. MORAL_REALISM: Moral facts exist and are accessible to reason.
    (Grounds: natural law, rational accessibility, universality)

P3. TELEOLOGICAL_FREEDOM: Freedom is ordered toward the good;
    choosing evil diminishes freedom.
    (Grounds: all freedom axioms, evil-as-self-undermining)

P4. LIBERTARIAN_FREE_WILL: Agents can genuinely choose otherwise.
    (Grounds: hell as self-exclusion, moral responsibility)

P5. TWO_TIER_CAUSATION: Primary and secondary causes do not compete.
    (Grounds: providence, creaturely dignity, divine action)
```

### Scriptural base (5 axioms)

```
S1. GOD_IS_LOVE: God's nature is love; love requires freedom.
    [1 Jn 4:8, Dt 30:19]
    (Grounds: love-requires-freedom, no predestination to hell)

S2. UNIVERSAL_SALVIFIC_WILL: God desires all to be saved.
    [1 Tim 2:4]
    (Grounds: no predestination, prevenient grace for all)

S3. LAW_ON_HEARTS: God inscribed moral law in human hearts.
    [Rom 2:14-15]
    (Grounds: natural law, divine grounding, universality)

S4. UNIVERSAL_PROVIDENCE: God governs all things, including
    free actions.
    [Mt 10:29, Is 46:10]
    (Grounds: providence, foreknowledge)

S5. SIN_SEPARATES: Grave sin breaks communion with God.
    [1 Jn 3:14-15, Rom 6:23]
    (Grounds: mortal sin → loss of grace, hell)
```

### Tradition base (5 axioms)

```
T1. GRACE_IS_NECESSARY_AND_TRANSFORMATIVE: Humans cannot do
    saving good without grace, and grace actually transforms.
    [Orange 529, Trent Session 6]
    (Grounds: anti-Pelagianism, grace bootstrapping, justification)

T2. GRACE_PRESERVES_FREEDOM: Divine initiative and human freedom
    are compatible; cooperation is real but grace-enabled.
    [Trent Session 6]
    (Grounds: grace-freedom compatibility, cooperation in justification)

T3. SACRAMENTAL_EFFICACY: Sacraments are effective signs that
    confer what they signify.
    [Trent Session 7]
    (Grounds: baptism confers justification)

T4. CONSCIENCE_BINDS: The certain judgment of reason about the
    good obliges the agent to follow it.
    [Aquinas, Vatican II DH §3]
    (Grounds: all conscience axioms)

T5. EVIL_IS_PRIVATION: Evil is the absence of due good, not a
    positive reality.
    [Augustine, Aquinas ST I q.49]
    (Grounds: causation asymmetry, God not cause of evil, permission ≠ causation)
```

---

## What this reduction reveals

1. **The philosophical base (P1-P5) is the most denomination-specific.**
   A Protestant would keep S1-S5, might keep T1 (modified) and T4,
   but would likely reject P1 (hylomorphism) and modify P3-P5.

2. **The Scriptural base (S1-S5) is broadly shared across Christianity.**
   Almost all Christians accept these five. The disagreements live
   in HOW they're interpreted — which is exactly what T1-T5 specify.

3. **The Tradition base (T1-T5) is where Catholic-Protestant divergence
   lives.** Drop T1 and you get Pelagianism. Modify T1 (forensic not
   transformative) and you get Luther. Drop T3 and you get Baptist
   theology. T1-T5 ARE Catholicism in 5 axioms.

4. **The applied axioms (Category D) should all be derivable** from
   P+S+T. This is the next project: actually proving the derivations
   in Lean.
